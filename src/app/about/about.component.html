The rating levels are progressive, ranging from 2.0 to 5.5. The higher level demands more advanced skills. A set of skills are defined and associated at each rating level. Each skill can be individually rated using academic grading scale ABCD. The rating result is shown in user-friendly Report Card format.

Skill Level Total Skills Description Equivalency
(just for fun)
2.0 10 Beginner Pre K
2.5 12 Beginner Kindergarten
3.0 11 Intermediate Middle School
3.5 18 Intermediate High School
4.0 22 Advanced Bachelor
4.5 19 Advanced Master
5.0 or higher 18 Professional PhD

For more information about the definition of Player Skill Ratings, please visit https://usapickleball.org/tournaments/tournament-player-ratings/player-skill-rating-definitions/
